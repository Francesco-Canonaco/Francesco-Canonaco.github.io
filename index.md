# Bio
<p style="text-align: justify;">
  I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Milano-Bicocca and a member of the <a href="http://www.mad.disco.unimib.it">MADLab</a> research group. My research investigates how Bayesian and Causal Networks can be applied to the study of the human microbiome, combining AI and statistical modeling to advance healthcare innovation.
