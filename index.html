Bio

I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Milano-Bicocca and a member of the MADLab research group. My research investigates how Bayesian and Causal Networks can be applied to the study of the human microbiome, combining AI and statistical modeling to advance healthcare innovation.

🏢 Co-founder @Minutia.AI
